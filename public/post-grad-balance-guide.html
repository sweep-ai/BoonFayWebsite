Section 07

The Rules That Cover Everything

Defaults that hold the standard intact through any week.

🔄
The Core Identity Shift
Stop Trying. Start Being.
The fit man trains on scheduled days — not when he feels like it
Systems and identity outlast motivation and willpower every time
The window is your mid-20s. What you build now compounds for decades
☀️
Every Day
Daily Non-Negotiables
🏋️ Train on scheduled days — no exceptions
🥩 Protein at every meal
😴 Protect weeknight sleep above almost everything
👥 Real connection with at least one person
📅
Every Week
Weekly Anchors
🏋️ 3 to 4 sessions completed
🛒 Sunday reset — groceries, prep, early night
🥡 Lunch to work at least 3 times
🧘 At least one genuine rest period
💼
When Work Gets Hard
Reduce Volume. Not Frequency.
📉 Cut sets — never cut sessions
🍳 Eat from prepped food — nutrition on autopilot
😴 Protect sleep above everything else
⚡ 25 min beats zero every single time
🎉
When Social Gets Heavy
Front-Load the Week
📅 Move training earlier in the week
🍽️ Eat before every event
🌅 Back to routine the morning after — no guilt
✅ One late night breaks nothing solid
